# Reading

- [Alfread](https://alfreadapp.com/) - a read-it-later app that helps you to actually read articles by setting goals and reminders. <nobr className="v-align"><i className="ri-apple-line"></i></nobr>

- [Braille Scanner](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/braille-scanner/id1517369095) - take a photo of a piece of paper with Braille on it and convert to text. <nobr className="v-align"><i className="ri-apple-line"></i></nobr>

- [Foliate](https://github.com/johnfactotum/foliate) - a simple and modern book reader for Linux. <nobr className="v-align"><i className="ri-ubuntu-line"></i> <i className="ri-open-source-line"></i></nobr>

- [Kill the Newsletter!](https://kill-the-newsletter.com/) - subscribe to a newsletter with a special email address and add a provided Atom feed. Each new email will become a new item in your RSS feed. <nobr className="v-align"><i className="ri-html5-line"></i> <i className="ri-open-source-line"></i></nobr>

- [NetNewsWire](https://netnewswire.com/) - a small and stable feed reader. <nobr className="v-align"><i className="ri-apple-line"></i> <i className="ri-open-source-line"></i></nobr>
