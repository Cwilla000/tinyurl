# Images

- [CleanUp.pictures](https://cleanup.pictures/) - remove any object, people, text or defects from your pictures. <nobr className="v-align"><i className="ri-html5-line"></i> <i className="ri-open-source-line"></i></nobr>

- [edit.photo](https://edit.photo/) - a free online photo editor. No ads/cookies/registration. <nobr className="v-align"><i className="ri-html5-line"></i></nobr>

- [ImageMagick](https://imagemagick.org/script/index.php) - an open source free tool for creating, editing, composing or converting digital images of different formats. <nobr className="v-align"><i className="ri-device-line"></i> <i className="ri-open-source-line"></i></nobr>
  ```bash copy
  # merge images
  brew install imagemagick
  convert +append 1.jpg 2.jpg 3.jpg best.jpg

  # resizing
  convert -resize 50% img.png img_resized.png

  # convert 3rd page of PDF to JPG
  convert input.pdf[2] output.jpg
  ```

- [ImageOptim](https://imageoptim.com/mac) makes images load faster by applying advanced compression and removing invisible junk. <nobr className="v-align"><i className="ri-device-line"></i> <i className="ri-open-source-line"></i></nobr>

- [qView](https://interversehq.com/qview/) - image viewer designed with minimalism and usability in mind. No cluttered interface, just your image and a titlebar. <nobr className="v-align"><i className="ri-device-line"></i> <i className="ri-open-source-line"></i></nobr>
