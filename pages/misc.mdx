# Miscellaneous

- [Drive and Listen](https://driveandlisten.app/) - "drive" around cities while listening to their local radios. <nobr className="v-align"><i className="ri-html5-line"></i></nobr>

- [KeyPad](https://apps.apple.com/in/app/keypad-bluetooth-keyboard/id1491684442) - use your Mac's keyboard and trackpad as a bluetooth keyboard and mouse on a phone, tablet or TV.
  > Alternative: [Typeeto](https://mac.eltima.com/bluetooth-keyboard.html)

- [Mactracker](http://mactracker.ca/) provides detailed information on every Apple Macintosh, iPod, iPhone, and iPad ever made. <nobr className="v-align"><i className="ri-apple-line"></i></nobr>

- [RunCat](https://kyome.io/runcat/index.html) - the more CPU load is, the faster the cat runs. <nobr className="v-align"><i className="ri-apple-line"></i></nobr>
  <Image src="/runcat.gif" alt="Hello" width={311} height={170} />

- [Shadowmap](https://app.shadowmap.org) - visualize solar shadows and the Sun in 3D. Anywhere on Earth, anytime. <nobr className="v-align"><i className="ri-html5-line"></i></nobr>

- [SiteSucker](https://ricks-apps.com/osx/sitesucker/index.html) - an app that can download an entire website including images, PDFs, style sheets, and other files. <nobr className="v-align"><i className="ri-apple-line"></i> <i className="ri-money-dollar-circle-line"></i></nobr>

- [what3words](https://what3words.com/) - this app divides the world into 3 metre squares and gives each square a unique combination of three words which you can use to easily find and share exact locations. <nobr className="v-align"><i className="ri-html5-line"></i> <i className="ri-apple-line"></i> <i className="ri-android-line"></i></nobr>

- [WindowSnap](https://www.window-swap.com/Window) - open a new window somewhere in the world. Look through windows shared by real people from different countries.
