# Audio

- [Krisp](https://krisp.ai/) - AI-powered app that removes background noise and echo from meetings leaving only human voice. <nobr><i className="ri-apple-line"></i></nobr>
  > Alternative: [Utterly](https://www.utterly.app/)

- [Micdrop](https://getmicdrop.com) mutes your mic in any app with a global keyboard shortcut or a menu bar control. <nobr><i className="ri-apple-line"></i></nobr>

- [Recordia](https://sindresorhus.com/recordia) lets you record audio directly from the menu bar or with a global keyboard shortcut. <nobr><i className="ri-apple-line"></i></nobr>
  > Alternatives: macOS [voice memos](https://support.apple.com/guide/voice-memos/record-vmaa4b813415/mac) or [Piezo](https://rogueamoeba.com/piezo/) app.
